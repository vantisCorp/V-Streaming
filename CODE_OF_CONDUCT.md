# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Positive Examples of Behavior

- **Welcoming**: Greeting new members and making them feel included
- **Respectful**: Treating others with dignity and respect
- **Inclusive**: Welcoming diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- **Collaborative**: Working together constructively
- **Helpful**: Assisting others and sharing knowledge
- **Considerate**: Being mindful of different viewpoints and experiences
- **Empathetic**: Showing understanding and compassion

### Negative Examples of Behavior

- **Harassment**: Unwelcome comments, jokes, or advances
- **Discrimination**: Treating others unfairly based on protected characteristics
- **Derogatory language**: Using slurs, epithets, or dehumanizing language
- **Personal attacks**: Insults, name-calling, or ad hominem arguments
- **Unwelcome sexual attention**: Sexual comments or advances
- **Public shaming**: Publicly criticizing or humiliating others
- **Doxxing**: Publishing private information without consent
- **Other unethical or unprofessional conduct**

## Our Responsibilities

### Community Leaders

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of
acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in
response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive,
or harmful.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject
comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are
not aligned to this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation
decisions when appropriate.

### Project Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Enforcing this Code of Conduct
- Responding to reports of misconduct
- Taking appropriate action to address violations
- Maintaining a welcoming and inclusive community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when
an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. Examples of
representing our project or community include using an official project e-mail
address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed
representative at an online or offline event.

### Community Spaces Include

- Discord server
- GitHub repositories
- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Social media channels
- Events and meetups
- Any other official V-Streaming community spaces

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience any unacceptable behavior, please report it to:

**Primary Contact**
- **Email**: conduct@v-streaming.com
- **Discord**: DM @Moderators

**What to Include**
- Your contact information
- Names of individuals involved (if applicable)
- Description of the incident
- Any relevant evidence (screenshots, logs, etc.)

### Investigation Process

1. **Receipt**: We acknowledge receipt of your report within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: We investigate thoroughly and fairly
3. **Action**: We take appropriate action based on findings
4. **Follow-up**: We follow up with the reporter and the accused

### Consequences

Community leaders will take appropriate action based on the severity of the
violation:

#### Level 1: Warning
For minor violations or first-time offenders:
- Private warning
- Request to stop the behavior
- Educational resources provided

#### Level 2: Temporary Suspension
For more serious or repeated violations:
- Temporary suspension from community spaces
- Removal of offensive content
- Probationary period

#### Level 3: Permanent Ban
For severe violations or repeated offenses after warnings:
- Permanent ban from community spaces
- Removal from project
- Inability to contribute

### Appeals

If you believe you have been unfairly sanctioned, you may appeal:

**Appeal Process**
1. Submit appeal in writing to conduct@v-streaming.com
2. Include details of the sanction and why you believe it was unfair
3. We will review your appeal within 7 days
4. We will respond with our decision
